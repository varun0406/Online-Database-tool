Wrappers

Wrappers indicate that you want multiple words to be used by the same field.
  • Quotes, such as au:"morawetz, cathleen", will force the order of the words to matter.
  • Use of parentheses, such as ti:(quotient algebra), makes the search ignore the order of the words, and ensures that all the words are included in the field specified.
  • Square brackets, such as y:[2010 2020], are used to specify date ranges.

Operators

Boolean operators allow you to make more detailed searches. These operator names are case sensitive.
  • ti:math AND au:"morawetz, cathleen" returns results that have both math in the title and Cathleen Morawetz as an author. AND is the default operator, so this query could also be written as ti:math au:"morawetz, cathleen".
  • ti:math OR au:"morawetz, cathleen" returns results that have either math in the title or Cathleen Morawetz as an author.
  • ti:math NOT au:"morawetz, cathleen" returns results that have math in the title, but do not have Cathleen Morawetz as an author.
  • Wild card searches are also supported, such as au:"morawetz, c*".
